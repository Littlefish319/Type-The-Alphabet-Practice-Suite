FAQ

  • My keyboard won’t pop up on iPhone: Tap inside the practice area to focus typing.
  • How do I reset a run?: Press Enter (or use the on-screen restart action if available).
  • Where is my data stored?: By default it’s on your device. If you sign in, a synced copy is stored in the cloud so it can sync across devices.
  • How do I delete my account?: Open the App → Account → Delete Account.
